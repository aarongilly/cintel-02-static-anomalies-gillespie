## Custom Project

### Dataset
I used the `clinic_data_gillespie.csv` dataset, which shows heights and weights from an adult clinic.

### Signals
I based my signals around minimum and maximum heights and ages for adults.

### Experiments
I set the thresholds a couple of times above, below, and in the middle of the data to ensure the filtering was occuring.

### Results
The filtering works! The whole approach seems very straightforward and effective.

### Interpretation
Checks like this can help prevent real-world data anomalies from making into analytical datasets where they can corrupt, or worse yet - *believably alter* things like averages and sums. These sorts of anomaly detections are invaluable for having confidence to rely on any real-world system processing real-world data.
